## 3. Personal Project Retrospective

During the first sprint of this project, I learned how to organize and manage a project backlog using Jira. Creating backlog items and assigning story point estimates helped me understand how agile teams plan and prioritize work.

One challenge I encountered was structuring the backlog and documentation properly within Jira. After reviewing assignment feedback and course materials, I improved the organization of my project tracking items and project documentation.

Overall, this sprint helped me gain practical experience with agile project management tools. In future sprints, I would like to improve my estimation accuracy and continue refining how I track project progress using burndown charts and backlog management.
